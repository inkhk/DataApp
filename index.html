Mapping Tornadoes in North Carolina
in History

by Lauren Song

On Februrary 24th, 2016, at least three tornadoes were reported in Central North Carolina. Tornadoes are a threat to human life and responsible for millions of dollars worth of property damage each year in North Carolina. We collect data from Tornado History Project to represent the temporal and spatial distribution of tornadoes in North Carolina.

Tornadoes in North Carolina by Year

Do You Know?

North Carolina is ranked 23 with a tornado risk factor of 20.25 among states.

The risk assessment is based on dividing the square mileage of each state against the frequency of death, injury, number of tornadoes, and cost of damages for each state.
